About us

DB Title Solutions is a global services provider offering knowledge processing services to Title companies seeking operational effectiveness, greater flexibility and lower operating costs, Located in Riverside, CA

DB Title Solutions provides residential and commercial Title Search and Tax Certificate Solutions Nationwide across United States. Our mission is to help our customers successfully grow their business by providing quality and professional Title experiences. Our clients depend on our speed and in-depth understanding to close transactions with confidence.
DB Title Solutions values each and every transaction and provides superior service for all parties involved with the transaction from beginning to end.

Our Solutions are designed to support Title Insurance companies, Title & Settlement Agents & Vendor Management companies. We provide a suite of Title services, customized to your requirements and needs, using our state of the art Technology platform and well experienced and qualified Title Searchers and Processors. DB Title Solutions has an AUTOMATED, streamlined workflow to ensure that your orders show up in our production queue within minutes and taken up for processing. If you have RUSHES or need PRIORITY processing for your Top Customers, our workflow will track them in a separate QUEUE handled by a dedicated team.

You can send your orders in any format indicating the services that you need, along with the inputs and DB Title Solutions takes over from there. Our Order routing engine assigns these orders to the respective desks and handles Priority orders and Rushes in a separate queue. Once the processing is completed, our delivery engine assembles the products and seamlessly delivers the output to you in a matter of hours. We empower you with our state of the art dashboard which helps you with Online Real-time information on the production status anytime, anywhere.

We adopt the latest technologies, with regard to on-premise software or on the cloud, that are compatible with your business and ours. This helps to maintain a streamlined, uninterrupted flow of project-related data between your office location, anywhere in the world, and our delivery centers. By partnering with DB Title Solutions you can get access to the best resources, deep industry expertise, long-term support and innovative thinking that will accelerate your business operations, and give you a sustainable edge in your industry.
